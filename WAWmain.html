Welcome to Wheelwomen at Work!

Wheelwomen at Work: Mapping Women's Involvement in the Nineteenth-Century Bicycle Industry is a digital humanities project which documents the diverse ways American women engaged in the bicycle industry as inventors, factory workers, saleswomen and mechanics.
